What are they?

A baby monitor is a device with a camera that will stream a feed wirelessly to you. The camera comes with infrared and night vision camera so that you can keep an eye on your baby even in the dark, and also make sure they are the right temperature. The device will also stream sound, so if they start crying, you will be able to hear them even from far away.

How does it help?

The baby monitor will keep your baby safe and healthy by letting you keep an eye on them, even when they want to sleep and you need to cook a meal in the other room. This will not only take stress off, but give you more freedom from your baby. Looking after a small child is a ton or work, but this will give you time alone while maintaining a watchful and caring eye.

Why should you buy one?

$30 - $70 is our price range, which isn't a lot compared to many other things. This is a small price to pay for security and ease of mind.
